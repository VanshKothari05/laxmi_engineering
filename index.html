Our Extensive Product Line for Maximum Efficiency

Our extensive catalog covers everything a modern manufacturing facility needs to maintain precise thermal control. We specialize in industrial oil burners that are capable of burning light diesel oil (LDO), furnace oil (FO), and high-speed diesel (HSD) with maximum combustion efficiency. By ensuring complete combustion, our burners drastically reduce fuel consumption and minimize carbon emissions, allowing your facility to operate within strict environmental regulations while saving on energy costs.

In addition to our combustion systems, we manufacture heavy-duty centrifugal and axial air blowers. These industrial air blowers provide the essential draft and ventilation required for safe and effective furnace operations. Built with precision-balanced impellers and rugged casings, our blowers can handle high temperatures, corrosive gases, and heavy dust loads without faltering. We also produce induced draft (ID) and forced draft (FD) fans custom-sized for your specific boiler or furnace setup.

Advanced Heat Treatment and Melting Furnaces

Laxmi Engineering Works is widely recognized as a leading industrial furnace manufacturer. We design and build a variety of furnaces, including pallet furnaces, rotary furnaces, and pit furnaces, all tailored for specific heat treatment and melting applications. Our furnaces are lined with high-grade refractory materials to ensure minimal heat loss, and they are equipped with advanced temperature control panels that allow operators to maintain precise thermal profiles.

Whether your process involves annealing, hardening, tempering, or non-ferrous metal melting, our engineering team will calculate the exact thermal load requirements to deliver a furnace that performs flawlessly. We integrate our own high-efficiency burners into these furnaces, creating a perfectly matched system that guarantees optimal performance and longevity.

Supporting Equipment: Oil Heating Units, Filters, and Cyclones

To ensure your combustion systems run smoothly, we manufacture comprehensive oil heating and pumping units. These skid-mounted systems come complete with simplex or duplex filters, pre-heaters, and high-pressure pumps. By pre-heating heavy fuel oils to the correct viscosity before they reach the burner nozzle, these units ensure consistent atomization and stable flames, which is critical for uninterrupted industrial production.

Furthermore, our pollution control and dust extraction equipment, such as our highly efficient cyclone separators, help facilities maintain clean air standards. These cyclones effectively remove particulate matter from exhaust gases before they are released into the atmosphere. We also supply a wide range of industrial valves, including butterfly valves, gate valves, and pressure relief valves, designed to handle high temperatures and abrasive materials safely.
